# Contributing to Google Drive Service Accounts Utility

First of all, thank you for considering contributing to this project!

## Contribution Terms
By submitting a Pull Request, you agree that your contributions will be licensed under the project's **MIT License**. This ensures that the project remains open and free for everyone to use.

## How to Contribute
1. **Fork the repository** and create your branch from `main`.
2. **Describe your changes**: In your Pull Request, briefly explain what problem you are solving or what feature you are adding.
3. **Security First**: 
   * NEVER commit your service account JSON keys or any other credentials.
   * Ensure that any testing files containing sensitive data are included in `.gitignore`.
4. **Code Style**: Try to keep the code clean and follow the existing style of the project.

## Reporting Issues
If you found a bug or have a feature request, please open an **Issue**. Provide as much detail as possible, including steps to reproduce the bug.

Thank you for your support!
